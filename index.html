📖 SYSTEM GUIDE

[1/4] THE DOOMSDAY DEADLINE

You are fighting against a countdown clock. Choose a major real-world deadline date. The app displays exactly how many days you have left to reach it, forcing you to see time slipping away.

[2/4] YOUR SLEEP RESET HOUR

Everyone has a different sleep schedule. Pick a reset hour between 12:00 AM and 6:00 AM. This is your personal midnight. If you do not finish your mandatory tasks before this hour, you face point penalties.

[3/4] UNLOCKING REWARDS

You will list real-world rewards and their actual money cost. The app turns that price into an XP goal. You never spend your points; your hard work clears the milestone so you can claim the reward guilt-free.

[4/4] TASK RULES

Main and Side Tasks are mandatory. Finish them before your reset hour, or you lose points. Errands and Bonus Tasks are optional. Completed tasks stay crossed-out on your screen all day to keep you motivated.

SYSTEM MANUAL

💡 POSTPONE (PT) BUTTON

Next to mandatory tasks is a yellow [PT] button. Having a terrible day? Click it to safely skip the task. You avoid the penalty loss, but get 0 XP.

🔥 EARNING TOKENS

You start with 3 PT tokens. Complete all mandatory tasks for 3 consecutive days to earn a "Perfect Streak" and get +1 free token.

❄️ REST / SICK DAY MODE

Turn this on in the Control Panel if you get sick or go on holiday. It freezes all penalties completely until you turn it off.
