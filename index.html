📖 QUEST GUIDE

[1/2] THE MANDATORY CORE

🔥 Main Quests are heavy focus blocks (e.g., Study Session, Office Project).

🛠️ Side Quests are structural habits (e.g., 10-Min Walk, Read 10 Pages).

Both are mandatory. Missing them before your daily reset hour will cost you heavy point penalties.

[2/2] OPTIONAL WINS

📜 Errands are chores you need to remember (e.g., Water Plants, Pay Bills).

⭐ Bonus Quests are wellness wins (e.g., Protein Shake, Cut Nails).

Both are optional. They grant extra points, but failing to do them carries absolutely zero penalties.

📖 SYSTEM GUIDE

[1/3] THE DOOMSDAY DEADLINE

You are fighting a countdown clock. Choose a major real-world deadline date (e.g., Final Exams, Project Launch). The app displays exactly how many days you have left, forcing you to see time slipping away.

[2/3] YOUR SLEEP RESET HOUR

Everyone has a different sleep schedule. Pick a reset hour between 12:00 AM and 6:00 AM. This is your personal midnight where the app rolls over to the next day.

[3/3] UNLOCKING REWARDS

List your real-world rewards and rank them. Earning XP pushes you to new Levels. As you level up, you naturally unlock your rewards from smallest treat to ultimate prize.

SYSTEM MANUAL

💡 POSTPONE (PT) BUTTON

Next to mandatory quests is a yellow [PT] button. Having a terrible day? Click it to safely skip the quest. You avoid the penalty loss, but get 0 XP.

🔥 EARNING TOKENS

You start with 3 PT tokens. Complete all mandatory quests for 3 consecutive days to earn a "Perfect Streak" and get +1 free token.

❄️ REST / SICK DAY MODE

Turn this on in the Control Panel if you get sick or go on holiday. It freezes all penalties completely until you turn it off.
