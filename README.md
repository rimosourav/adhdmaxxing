## 🎮 How It Works (The Core System Rules)

The system has been re-engineered from the ground up to completely eliminate economic guesswork. There are no arbitrary money-to-XP conversions. The software balances itself automatically based on your real-world calendar constraints.

### ⚔️ The Quest Board Architecture

* **🔥 Main Quests (Heavy Focus Blocks):** High-priority focus targets (+50 XP). *Mandatory.*
* **🛠️ Side Quests (Habit Formations):** Key daily structural routines (+20 XP). *Mandatory.*
* *The Penalty Mechanic:* Failing to check off a Mandatory Quest before your Sleep Reset hour triggers a severe rollover penalty drop ($-1.5\times$ the quest's value).


* **📜 Errands (Chores & Reminders):** Single or interval events (+75 XP). *Optional.*
* **⭐ Bonus Quests (Wellness Wins):** Extra dopamine triggers (+15 XP). *Optional.*
* *The Protection Mechanic:* Optional quests yield high reward points but carry zero penalties if skipped.



### utilities Utility Operational Triggers

* **🔀 Postpone (PT) Tokens:** Having a critical mental health day? Expend a PT Token to safely skip a mandatory quest. It marks the quest cleared, grants 0 XP, but completely freezes the rollover penalty. You start with 3 tokens.
* **🔥 Perfect Streaks:** Clear 100% of your mandatory quests for 3 consecutive days to score a Perfect Streak streak multiplier and receive +1 free PT Token.
* **🌙 Sleep Reset Anchor:** Not everyone operates on a standard corporate schedule. Set your "System Reset Hour" between 12:00 AM and 6:00 AM so late-night grinds are credited to the correct calendar block.
* **❄️ Stasis Mode:** Going on holiday or dealing with a severe illness? Flip the **Rest / Sick Day** switch in the Control Panel to completely freeze your profile state and bypass all incoming penalties.

## 🚀 The Multi-Year Endgame Loop

### 📈 The Dynamic Hypothetical Yield Algorithm

The system completely handles the game balance for you. When you launch a new real-world chapter, you simply brain-dump your desired rewards and use the tactile up/down controls to rank them from **Easiest Treat** to **Ultimate Prize**.

The app scans your active mandatory quests, counts the exact days remaining on your countdown clock, and maps out a customized **Exponential Leveling Track** ($XP = 100 \times (Level - 1)^{1.5}$). It calculates your maximum potential yield and scales the milestones so that your ultimate prize is always perfectly achievable, but requires serious consistency right up to the final day.

### 🛡️ The 9-Cycle Elite Pyramid

Your character level resets down to Level 1 at the end of every Doomsday to ensure you constantly experience the rapid, addictive leveling speeds of the early game. However, your **lifetime survival** is tracked permanently by your Cycle Counter, which scales your Avatar Frame with glowing pulsing CSS color grading via a narrowing pyramid structure:

* **NOVICE I - IV** (Cycles 1 - 4): Gunmetal Gray Border
* **AWAKENED I - III** (Cycles 5 - 7): Glowing Neon Blue Aura
* **VETERAN I - II** (Cycles 8 - 9): Pulsing Amethyst Purple Aura
* **ELITE** (Cycle 10+): Blazing Radiant Gold Aura

### 🏆 New Game Plus (Annual Victory Reset)

Upon successfully conquering your 9th milestone cycle and unlocking the legendary **Elite** tier, the system rewards you with the **Annual Victory Celebration Screen**. From there, you can choose to enter **New Game Plus (Year 2)**, resetting the progression tracks back to baseline while adding a permanent master survivor crest to your avatar aura to showcase your lifetime achievement.

## 📲 Installation & Native Mobile Deployment

Because this program executes completely in-browser utilizing sandboxed HTML5 Local Storage architecture, you do not need an App Store to turn it into a native-feeling mobile app:

1. Save your updated code text as an `index.html` file.
2. Send the file to your smartphone (via AirDrop, email, iCloud, Google Drive, or Discord).
3. Open the file inside your mobile browser (Safari for iOS, Chrome for Android).
4. **Pin it to your phone:**
* **iOS / Safari:** Tap the **Share** icon at the bottom, scroll down, and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
* **Android / Chrome:** Tap the **3-dot menu** icon at the top right, and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.


5. Launch the app directly from your phone's home screen icon. It will open in seamless, edge-to-edge full screen, function completely offline, and maintain your state flawlessly.
