⚡ ADHDmaxxing: System Awakened
Turn your real life into a ruthless, high-stakes RPG. > ADHDmaxxing is a zero-friction, fully offline gamified productivity dashboard built entirely in a single HTML file. Fight the Doomsday clock, earn XP to unlock real-world rewards, and manage your daily quests with absolute clarity. No ads, no tracking, no subscriptions—just you against your own executive dysfunction.

🎮 How It Works (The Rules)
The System is built on strict accountability and real-world economy. You don't just check off boxes; you survive the day.

🔥 Mandatory Quests (Main & Side): The core of your day (e.g., Study Session, Gym). Rule: Missing these before your daily reset time triggers a massive XP penalty.

⭐ Optional Quests (Errands & Bonus): Extra wins (e.g., Take Vitamins, Water Plants). Rule: Grants bonus XP, but skipping them carries zero penalties.

💛 Postpone (PT) Tokens: Having a terrible mental health day? Use a PT token to safely skip a mandatory quest without taking an XP penalty. You start with 3.

🔥 Perfect Streaks: Complete all mandatory quests for 3 consecutive days to earn +1 free PT Token.

🌙 Custom Reset Hour: Not everyone sleeps at midnight. Set your "System Reset" to 3 AM or 4 AM so late-night grinds actually count for today.

🏆 The Real-World Economy: Link your XP directly to real money (e.g., $10 Iced Coffee = 1000 XP). Your hard work clears the milestone so you can buy your treats guilt-free.

⏳ The Doomsday Clock: Set a major real-world deadline (Final Exams, Project Launch). The dashboard constantly displays the days remaining, forcing you to see time slipping away.

🚀 Installation & How to Use (No App Store Needed)
Because this app runs on pure Local Storage via your browser, you don't need to download it from an app store. You can turn it into a native-feeling app on your phone in 30 seconds:

Download the index.html file from this repository.

Send it to your phone (via email, Discord, iCloud, Google Drive, etc.) and open it in your mobile browser (Safari or Chrome).

Install it:

iOS / Safari: Tap the Share icon at the bottom, scroll down, and tap "Add to Home Screen".

Android / Chrome: Tap the 3-dot menu at the top right, and tap "Add to Home Screen".

Play: Open the new icon on your home screen. It will run in full screen, completely offline, and save all your data locally on your device!
